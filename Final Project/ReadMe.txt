PizzaPanic basic instructions

Help Farmer John feed all the hungry animals before the time runs out! You have 30 seconds to feed the animals a piece of pizza and earn 5 points each. Score 45 points before the timer reaches 0 and Win! But be careful!!! Avoid running into the animals by jumping over them if you don't feed them any pizza. You will automatically lose if you run into one. Collect a soccer ball and automatically win!

Key commands:

J- jump
Space bar - shoot pizza
